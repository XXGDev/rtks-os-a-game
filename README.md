# rtks-os-a-game
A game about a fake operating system, made by xxgdev on the Godot game engine.\
Play at https://xxgstudios.netlify.app/games/rtks-os/ (web release only, at the moment)

### Features

* RTKS Office & RTKS Studio: Write documents with RTKS Documents, create spreadsheets in RTKS Sheets, and more.
* RTKS Browser: Browse the internet with this reliable browser.
* RTKS Music: Listen to catchy music picked/made by realTheKINGS.
* Personalize your PC: Personalize your desktop with a variety of wallpapers to choose.
* ...And many more you can find throughout the OS.

### What is RTKS and realTheKINGS?
RTKS is short for realTheKINGS, which is a community on Roblox and YouTube. It is known for its incompetence and an owner who doesn't manage their workers right. Most of the elements of the game are based on the different kinds of actions and 'punishments' the owner does, and also the way the owner behaves and talks.

Learn more about me at https://xxgstudios.netlify.app/
